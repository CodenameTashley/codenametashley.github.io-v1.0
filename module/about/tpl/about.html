About Me

Hello, I am Tashley Toocaram and a fresh graduate that was enrolled in a bachelor degree with honours in Applied Computing at the University of Mauritius. I joined the professional world of software development as a Junior Software Developer at CHB Technologies Ltd after my tertiary education.

This small web app is about introducing me and it was developed during my free time (after winning too many match in FIFA). It is also an interface to connect you guys with my different social network accounts.

I was introduced in the programming world by Mr. James Leong Mook Seng at the age of 17 and at that time I was writing codes in Pascal. And during my pre-university holidays, I developed small programs using Pascal just to keep doing what I always love doing; write codes.

Then I joined the University of Mauritius in August 2014 to pursue my studies in the Computing world. There, I was introduced to my all-time favorite programming language, Java and also other languages like PHP, JavaScript, etc.
Apart from the programming languages that was taught at the University, I also learned basic C#, Python, C++ and Kotlin.

In June 2015, I was in the second batch of the Smart Apps Internship Programme organised by Microsoft Student Partners (Mauritius), HM Rawat, Ceridian and Emtel. During the 6 weeks of the programme, we were given the task to create a Windows Phone App.

I started my 8 months traineeship at Infomil Mauritius Ltd under its annual programme named Programme d'encadrement that has the aim to provide Level 2 students of universities, the opportunity to work on a project under a professional environment. It was indeed an enriching experience to be surrounded by IT professionals who shared the maximum of their technical & non-technical skills and career tips with us.

Before entering in the final year of my BSc programme, along with a classmate, we worked on a project entitled A Web Based Audio Learning System using Drupal. The duration of this small project was also for 6 weeks.
